Experience Healing and Wellness

Ascent Physiotherapy is a comprehensive physiotherapy clinic based in Comox, BC. We provide a range of services, including Physiotherapy, Manual/Manipulative Therapy, IMS, Acupuncture, Radial Shockwave Therapy, and personalized exercise programs such as Personal Training. Our services cater to the residents of the Comox Valley and nearby areas.
